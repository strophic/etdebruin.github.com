1Password

Sep 8th, 2011

Too often when I use 1password to log into a site, I am faced with multiple logins for the same site. As the time monster would have it, I almost always choose the wrong password and the ‘Incorrect Password’ alarms start ringing.

Here is how you can make it better, 1Password:

Allow me to ‘x’ out the passwords that don’t work anymore (or incorrectly configured). Or,

Since you are able to see that the password is incorrect, why not present me with a choice to either keep the password or delete it?

You have a great product. You are fanatic. Do it.
